Hi there. I'm John Mason, a public equities investor, product manager, and aspiring entrepreneur.

I'm currently on sabbatical living in Seoul, South Korea. Most recently, I led two teams as a PM at Lithic, a card issuing infrastructure company directly integrated with Visa and Mastercard. Previously, I worked as a PM at Fluence, a grid-scale battery storage integrator, and at Optoro, a company building software to handle returned retail inventory.

Before that, I was part of Microsoft's corporate strategy team and the Boston Consulting Group.

I'm active on Twitter, mostly to interact with professional public equities investors. Right now, I'm focused on metallurgical coal equities, which I believe are undervalued relative to how persistently critical they will be for developing countries to grow and for the world to transition to new energy infrastructure.

Feel free to reach out via email. Cheers!
